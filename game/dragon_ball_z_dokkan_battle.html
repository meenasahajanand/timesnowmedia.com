How To Play

"Dragon Ball Z Dokkan Battle is a popular mobile game developed by Akatsuki Inc. in collaboration with Bandai Namco Entertainment. It combines the world of Dragon Ball Z with engaging gameplay mechanics, strategic team-building, and stunning visuals. Since its release in 2015, the game has amassed a large and dedicated fan base, offering an exciting experience for both Dragon Ball fans and mobile gamers alike.

Dragon Ball Z Dokkan Battle takes players on a journey through the iconic Dragon Ball Z storyline, allowing them to relive key moments and battles from the series. The game follows the adventures of Goku, the main protagonist, and other beloved characters as they defend Earth from various powerful villains. Players assume the role of a Time Patroller, tasked with restoring the timeline and preserving the Dragon Ball Z universe.

The gameplay of Dragon Ball Z Dokkan Battle combines elements of puzzle-solving and team strategy. Players form a team of powerful characters and engage in strategic battles against enemies using a unique puzzle-based combat system. The battles are turn-based, and players must link together colored Ki Spheres to perform attacks, unleash special skills, and activate powerful Super Attacks.

During battles, players must strategically select Ki Spheres of the same color as their characters to fill their Ki meters. When the Ki meter is fully charged, players can launch powerful attacks that deal massive damage to enemies. The selection and placement of Ki Spheres require careful planning, as players must consider character types, affiliations, and the unique abilities of their team members to maximize damage output and defense.

One of the key features of Dragon Ball Z Dokkan Battle is the wide range of characters available for players to collect and assemble into teams. Characters are obtained through summoning using in-game currency or by participating in various events and rewards. Each character possesses different stats, abilities, and Leader Skills that can greatly impact the outcome of battles.

The characters in the game are categorized based on their rarity, represented by different colors. Higher rarity characters generally have superior stats, more powerful abilities, and unique Super Attacks that reflect their iconic moves from the Dragon Ball Z series. Players can strengthen their characters by leveling them up, unlocking new abilities, and performing special Awakening processes to unlock their full potential.

Building your dream team:

Rarity and character types: Learn about the different rarity levels and character types in Dokkan Battle. Understand their strengths, weaknesses, and how to form a balanced team.

Link skills and passive abilities: Explore the importance of Link Skills and passive abilities in battles. Maximize the synergy between your characters to unlock powerful bonuses and unleash devastating attacks.

Awakening and Dokkan Awakening: Discover the process of awakening your characters to unlock their full potential. Dive into Dokkan Awakening to further strengthen your fighters and unleash their ultimate power.

Battle system overview: Familiarize yourself with the turn-based battle system in Dokkan Battle, including attacking, defending, and using character-specific super attacks.

Ki Spheres and super attacks: Understand the significance of Ki Spheres and how to strategically match them to unleash character super attacks. Learn to manipulate the Ki board for optimal results.

Elemental advantage and disadvantage: Discover the elemental advantage and disadvantage system in battles. Exploit type matchups to deal increased damage and minimize incoming damage.

Events, quests, and challenges:

Story quests: Embark on an exciting journey through the Dragon Ball Z storyline by participating in Story Quests. Relive iconic moments and earn valuable rewards along the way.

Event quests and limited-time events: Take part in event quests, which offer exclusive rewards and challenge stages. Engage in limited-time events for rare character drops, special items, and event-specific currency.

Dokkan events and boss battles: Prepare for intense battles in Dokkan Events, where formidable bosses await. Develop strategies, exploit weaknesses, and assemble a powerful team to emerge victorious.

Friend system and guest characters: Utilize the Friend System to add powerful allies to your team and borrow their leaders' skills. Strategically choose guest characters that synergize with your team composition.

World tournament: Compete in the thrilling World Tournament against other players worldwide. Aim for high rankings, earn rewards, and prove your skills as the ultimate Dokkan Battle warrior.

Community interaction: Engage with the Dokkan Battle community through social media, forums, and online groups. Share tips, strategies, and updates, and participate in community-driven events

With the knowledge and strategies provided in this guide, you're well-equipped to dive into the exhilarating world of Dragon Ball Z Dokkan Battle. Unleash your Saiyan power, build a formidable team of fighters, and relive the iconic moments of the Dragon Ball Z series. Embrace the thrilling battles, collect legendary characters, and conquer challenging events. Whether you're a long-time fan of the Dragon Ball franchise or new to the world of Dokkan Battle, this mobile game promises an epic adventure filled with intense battles and unforgettable experiences. Prepare to go Super Saiyan and become the strongest warrior in the Dragon Ball universe. Good luck, and may your journey be legendary!"
