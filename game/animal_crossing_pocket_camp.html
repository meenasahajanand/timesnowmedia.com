How To Play

One of the popular simulation games from Nintendo is Animal Crossing Pocket Camp which has been doing rounds more recently thanks to the rising player base since its original launch. It’s a great experience to play the game on mobile even though the game doesn’t reflect every aspect of the main ??Animal Crossing’ game.

But it has certain similarities that players around the world love. Besides, the mobile version of the gameplay comes with a unique gameplay loop. Although the game retains the same gameplay and style, it has simple objectives. Let’s understand the gameplay and discuss some helpful tips that will give you the best gaming experience.

Animal Crossing Pocket Camp is one of the hottest gaming titles from Nintendo and everyone should check it out. While the original Animal Crossing franchise was developed for PCs and consoles, they were introduced to mobile platforms and doing great. The gameplay is simple and it revolves around building an environment depending on your preferences.

You get the liberty to experience real-life happenings while molding them according to what you like. The best part is that you can experience almost similar gameplay you found on the original title with all accessibilities and customizations. You can make your theme park, build cafes, and enjoy festivals. Besides, you can design your campsite while decorating it with exclusive items.

Below listed are general tips that help you get a better grip on the game while improving your gaming experience.

1. Don't avoid villager requests

It is wise to farm villager requests several times a day. When traveling to different recreational zones, you will find villagers wandering here and there. You have to complete three requests in exchange for rewards from the villagers. Completing villager requests is the only way to increase your friendship level with other characters. As such, it helps you unlock crafting recipes and level up the game. Also, the requests give you lots of crafting materials that you may need later on. Hence, interact with the villagers to complete their requests and earn rewards.

2. Make sure to have a varied campsite population

After increasing your friendship level with villagers, you can request them to move into your campsite. Or, you can keep them until you think about replacing them. However, before you invite any villagers to your campsite, know their type and species. That’s because they reward you when you complete tasks for them. The bonus depends on the species and type. So, when you have similar species in the campsite, you will have a surplus of one material and a deficit of another. Keep that in mind when expanding your campsite.

3. Get hold of the unique fruit trees

For every player, there are 2 fruit trees that they can find at Lost Lure Creek. But they are different from the others. It means the location of the fruit trees for one player will differ from that of another player’s location. Besides, there are chances that you may have twice the production of those fruit trees. Next, you have to put the fruits in the Market Box every day. This allows others to know that you are having surplus fruits.

4. Spend the leaf ticket with caution

Although having leaf tickets is easy, you need to prioritize them when it comes to spending them. The best way to spend leaf tickets is to buy more crafting spaces. Another best way to use the leaf tickets is to expand your storage. As you level up, you will gain inventory slots up to 150.

Animal Crossing Pocket Camp comes with excellent features in terms of every aspect. Moreover, you can expect a lot of intuitive things in the game because it comes from Nintendo. There is no shortage of entertainment when it comes to having a sheer gameplay experience of a specific genre.

Because of its popularity, the game has already a massive fan base. After the launch of the mobile version, it brought in more players. Overall, the game is an exciting game that you can’t afford to miss. If you are yet to check out the Animal Crossing franchise, maybe Pocket Camp is the best way to start. Therefore, go ahead and build your beautiful virtual world.
