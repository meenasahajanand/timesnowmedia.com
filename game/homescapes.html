How To Play

"Homescapes is a delightful and immersive mobile game that combines elements of puzzle solving, home renovation, and engaging storytelling. Developed by Playrix Games, the creators of the highly popular Gardenscapes, Homescapes offers a unique and addictive gameplay experience that has captivated millions of players worldwide.

In Homescapes, players follow the endearing protagonist, Austin the butler, as he returns to his childhood home, which has seen better days. The once beautiful mansion is now in desperate need of restoration and redecoration. It's up to the players to help Austin renovate the house by completing a series of challenging match-3 puzzles.

The game features a compelling narrative that unfolds as players progress through various levels. Players not only solve puzzles but also interact with a host of colorful characters, including Austin's parents, loyal friends, and mischievous pets. The engaging storyline keeps players invested in Austin's journey to restore his home and uncover the secrets hidden within its walls.

The core gameplay of Homescapes revolves around completing match-3 puzzles. Players must swap and match various colored pieces on a grid to create matches of three or more. Each level presents unique objectives, such as collecting specific items, removing obstacles, or spreading carpets. By successfully completing levels, players earn stars, which are then used to complete tasks within the mansion renovation process.

To play with a higher score, here are some tips and strategies:

Understand the match-3 mechanics: The core gameplay involves matching three or more identical pieces (candies, fruits, etc.) to clear them from the board. Take the time to understand the different power-ups and how they can be created by making larger matches or combining special pieces.

Create power-ups: Look for opportunities to create power-ups by making matches of four or more pieces. These power-ups can be incredibly helpful in clearing difficult levels. Match four pieces in a row to create a Rocket, or match four in a square to make a Paper Plane. Matching five pieces in an L or T shape creates a Bomb, and matching five pieces in a row forms a Rainbow Ball.

Combine power-ups: Combining power-ups can have a powerful effect on the board. For example, combining two Rockets clears a row and a column in a cross shape. Combining a Paper Plane with a power-up clears several pieces in a cross shape and activates the Paper Plane's ability.

Prioritize objectives: Each level in Homescapes has specific objectives, such as collecting a certain number of items or removing obstacles. Focus on completing these objectives to progress through the game. Try to make matches near the required items or obstacles to clear them efficiently.

Use boosters wisely: Boosters are special items that can help you overcome challenging levels. They can be earned as rewards or purchased with in-game currency. Save your boosters for levels that are particularly difficult or where you're running out of moves.

Make use of helpers: As you progress in the game, you'll unlock various helpers such as the Hammer, Rainbow Blast, and TNT. These tools can assist you in removing specific obstacles or clearing difficult areas. Use them strategically to maximize their impact.

Take advantage of daily rewards and events: Homescapes often offers daily rewards and special events. Make sure to log in daily to claim these rewards and participate in events. They can provide you with additional boosters, coins, or other helpful items.

Be patient: Some levels can be challenging, but don't get discouraged. Keep trying different strategies and approaches. Homescapes can require a bit of patience and persistence, but with practice, you'll improve your skills and progress through the game.

Remember, the main focus of Homescapes is the storyline and home renovation aspect, so enjoy the game at your own pace while having fun decorating Austin's childhood home. Good luck!"
