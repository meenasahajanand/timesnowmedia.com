The objective of Mahjong Triple 3D is to clear the board by matching identical tiles in pairs. However, not all tiles are accessible from the beginning, as some may be blocked by other tiles. Players must strategize and plan their moves carefully to uncover and match the available pairs.

One of the difficulties players may face is encountering blocked tiles that cannot be matched immediately. This can create obstacles and limit the available moves. To overcome this challenge, players should focus on removing tiles that are blocking the desired pairs. Look for opportunities to clear the path by eliminating tiles that are adjacent to the blocked ones.

In certain game modes, Mahjong Triple 3D imposes time constraints, adding a sense of urgency to the gameplay. Players must clear the board within a specified time limit to achieve a high score or complete the level. To overcome this challenge, prioritize matching easily accessible pairs first, rather than spending too much time searching for specific pairs. Utilize the hint feature when stuck to save time and make efficient moves.

As players progress through the game, they will encounter more complex tile arrangements. The tiles may be stacked in multiple layers or arranged in intricate patterns, making it challenging to identify and match pairs. To tackle this difficulty, focus on clearing the topmost layer of tiles first, as it opens up opportunities to access the tiles underneath. Plan ahead and consider the consequences of removing certain tiles to avoid blocking potential matches.

In some levels, players may have limited moves available to complete the board. Running out of moves before clearing all the tiles can result in a failed level. To overcome this challenge, analyze the board and prioritize removing tiles that free up the most options for matching pairs. Look for tiles that are surrounded by other tiles or those that are blocking multiple pairs simultaneously. Make strategic decisions to maximize your available moves.

As players advance through the game, the difficulty level gradually increases with more complex tile arrangements and challenging layouts. This can pose a greater challenge in finding matches and completing levels. To cope with this difficulty, take your time to observe the board and plan your moves before making them.
