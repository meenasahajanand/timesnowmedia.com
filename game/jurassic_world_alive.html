How To Play

Jurassic World Alive is an exhilarating mobile game that combines the excitement of exploring the real world with the thrill of encountering and collecting augmented reality (AR) dinosaurs. Inspired by the Jurassic World film franchise, the game offers a unique and immersive experience that has captivated players worldwide.

The game's content revolves around the following key features:

AR dinosaur encounters: Jurassic World Alive leverages augmented reality technology to bring dinosaurs into the real world. Players can explore their surroundings, using their device's camera to spot and capture virtual dinosaurs that appear as if they're right in front of them. This immersive experience allows players to fulfill their childhood dreams of interacting with these prehistoric creatures.

Dinosaur collection: As players explore different locations, they have the opportunity to encounter a wide variety of dinosaurs, including fan favorites from the Jurassic World franchise and newly discovered species. Each dinosaur has its own unique characteristics, such as strengths, weaknesses, and abilities, making the collection aspect of the game engaging and strategic.

Battle arena: Jurassic World Alive features a competitive Battle Arena where players can test their dinosaur teams against other players worldwide. By strategically selecting and deploying their dinosaurs in turn-based battles, players aim to defeat their opponents and climb the ranks. The dynamic battles require tactical thinking, team synergy, and knowledge of the dinosaurs' abilities, adding depth to the gameplay experience.

DNA collection and evolution: To enhance their dinosaur collection and improve their battling capabilities, players need to collect DNA samples. This is achieved by successfully capturing dinosaurs in the wild or participating in special events. Accumulated DNA can be used to evolve dinosaurs, unlocking new abilities, increasing their power, and even creating hybrid dinosaurs with unique traits.

Exploration and exploration events: Jurassic World Alive encourages players to venture out into the real world to discover new dinosaurs. Exploration events provide opportunities for players to find specific dinosaurs in designated areas or during limited-time events. This promotes outdoor exploration, social interaction, and the excitement of tracking down rare and elusive creatures.

In-game features and events: The game offers various in-game features and events to keep players engaged and entertained. These include special themed events, community challenges, weekly tournaments, and limited-time offers. These features provide additional goals and rewards, fostering a sense of progression and excitement.

The game's augmented reality technology allows players to see and interact with dinosaurs in their own environment, blurring the lines between the virtual and real world. This unique experience has captured the imagination of players, creating a strong sense of wonder and fascination.

The game taps into the enduring popularity of the Jurassic World film franchise, which has a dedicated fan base worldwide. Jurassic World Alive allows fans to engage with their favorite dinosaurs and immerse themselves in the Jurassic World universe, creating a strong emotional connection.

The game's Battle Arena fosters a competitive environment where players can test their skills against others. This competitive aspect, combined with the ability to join alliances and interact with fellow players, encourages social engagement and friendly competition.

Collection and Evolution Mechanics: The thrill of collecting and evolving dinosaurs appeals to players' desire for progression and personalization. The ability to create unique hybrid dinosaurs adds depth to the gameplay and allows players to create their own powerful and distinctive teams.

The game developers consistently introduce new content, including new dinosaurs, features, and events. This regular stream of updates keeps the game fresh and exciting, providing ongoing challenges and opportunities for discovery.

Here's a guide on how to play the game and the surprises that await you:

Exploration and dinosaur encounters: The game encourages you to venture out into the real world to explore different locations, such as parks, neighborhoods, and landmarks. As you move around, you can use your device's GPS and camera to spot and capture augmented reality dinosaurs that appear in your surroundings. Keep an eye out for dinosaurs of various rarities and species, including both familiar faces from the Jurassic World films and newly discovered creatures.

Drone capture: When you encounter a dinosaur, you can deploy a drone to get a closer look and capture it. The drone allows you to aim and launch darts to collect DNA samples from the dinosaur. The better your aim, the more DNA you collect, which is crucial for evolving and improving your dinosaurs.

Dinosaur collection and evolution: As you capture dinosaurs and collect their DNA, you can use it to evolve and enhance your creatures. By combining DNA samples from different dinosaurs, you can create powerful hybrid dinosaurs with unique abilities. Experiment with different combinations to build a formidable team of dinosaurs.

Battle arena: Engage in thrilling battles against other players in the Battle Arena. Build a team of your strongest dinosaurs and strategically deploy them in turn-based battles. Each dinosaur has its own set of abilities, and choosing the right ones at the right time is key to victory. Earn trophies and climb the ranks to unlock greater rewards and face more challenging opponents.

Exploration events and special supply drops: The game offers special events that provide unique opportunities for exploration and encounters. These events can include increased chances to find rare dinosaurs, limited-time quests, or themed challenges. Take part in these events to make exciting discoveries and earn special rewards. Additionally, keep an eye out for special supply drops, which contain valuable resources, such as additional darts for your drone and in-game currency.

Surprise encounters and discoveries: While exploring, you may encounter surprises and unexpected encounters. This can include rare or legendary dinosaurs appearing in unexpected locations, the discovery of hidden nests, or encountering special event-exclusive creatures. These surprises add an element of excitement and unpredictability to the gameplay, encouraging you to continue exploring and searching for hidden gems.

Alliance and social interaction: Join or create an alliance to connect and interact with other players. Collaborate with alliance members to take on cooperative missions, share strategies, and compete in alliance-based tournaments. Social interaction adds a sense of community and camaraderie to the game, enhancing the overall experience.

Regular updates and new content: Jurassic World Alive regularly receives updates that introduce new features, dinosaurs, events, and gameplay improvements. The developers strive to keep the game fresh and engaging, ensuring that there are always new surprises and experiences awaiting players.

Playing jurassic world alive is an adventure filled with surprises, discoveries, and strategic battles. Immerse yourself in the world of dinosaurs, explore your surroundings, and collect powerful creatures to create the ultimate dinosaur collection. Prepare for unexpected encounters, exciting events, and the joy of seeing augmented reality dinosaurs come to life in your own environment. Get ready to embark on an exhilarating journey and become a skilled dinosaur trainer in Jurassic World Alive.
