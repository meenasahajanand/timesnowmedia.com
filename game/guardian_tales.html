How To Play

Guardian Tales is a mobile action-adventure game developed by Kong Studios and published by Kakao Games. It combines elements of classic pixel art RPGs with modern gameplay mechanics, creating a unique and engaging experience for players. The game's charming pixel graphics, captivating storyline, and challenging gameplay have garnered a dedicated fan base since its release.

In Guardian Tales, players embark on a quest-filled journey through various imaginative worlds, battling enemies, solving puzzles, and uncovering secrets along the way. The game features a mix of single-player story content and multiplayer game modes, allowing players to team up with friends or join guilds to take on challenging dungeons and bosses.

One important aspect of Guardian Tales is the home system, where players can decorate and customize their own in-game homes. Improving your home's love level is crucial as it unlocks additional features, rewards, and interactions with various characters. Here are some tips to quickly improve your home love in Guardian Tales:

Complete Story Missions: Progressing through the game's main storyline will often unlock new furniture items and decorations for your home. Completing story missions is a great way to earn experience points for your home and increase its love level.

Daily Quests and Achievements: Guardian Tales offers daily quests and achievements that provide rewards upon completion. These rewards often include furniture pieces, which can be used to decorate your home and increase its love level.

Participate in Events: The game regularly introduces limited-time events with unique rewards. These events often include exclusive furniture items that can boost your home's love level. Make sure to participate in these events and complete the associated tasks to maximize your progress.

Upgrade and Place Furniture: Each furniture item in Guardian Tales has its own love value, which contributes to your home's overall love level. Upgrading your furniture using resources and in-game currency will increase its love value. Be strategic in placing your furniture to optimize the love points gained.

Visit Other Players' Homes: Guardian Tales allows players to visit each other's homes, offering a social aspect to the game. Visiting other players' homes not only provides inspiration for your own decor but also rewards you with additional love points for your home. Interacting with objects in their homes, such as watering plants or feeding pets, can grant extra love points.

Complete Home Requests: Various characters in Guardian Tales will occasionally request specific furniture or decorations for their homes. Fulfilling these requests not only earns you rewards but also increases your home's love level.

Daily Login Rewards: Logging into Guardian Tales every day grants you login rewards. These rewards often include materials or currency that can be used to upgrade your furniture and improve your home's love level.

Use Love Boosters: Love Boosters are consumable items that provide a temporary boost to your home's love points. They can be obtained through events, achievements, or in-game purchases. Save them for when you need an extra push to increase your home's love level quickly.

Complete Side Quests: Guardian Tales features numerous side quests that offer additional rewards, including furniture items. These quests often involve exploring hidden areas, solving puzzles, or defeating powerful enemies. Completing side quests not only enhances your overall gameplay experience but also contributes to your home's love level.

Participate in Guild Activities: Joining a guild in Guardian Tales unlocks various benefits, including guild activities and rewards. Participating in guild missions, raids, and other cooperative activities can grant you additional resources that can be used to improve your home's love level.

By following these tips and staying active in the game, players can quickly improve their home love in Guardian Tales. Remember to have fun exploring the game's rich world, interacting with its quirky characters, and unleashing your creativity in decorating your home.
