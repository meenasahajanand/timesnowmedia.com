How To Play

Have you ever imagined playing to your heart's content in a virtual digital entertainment world? Cyber Surfer: Beat&Skateboard gives you the opportunity to do so, a music and skateboard-based game that uses leading-edge virtual reality technology to allow you to control your character as you soar through the air. In this thrilling world of digital entertainment, you will experience challenges and thrills like never before. Are you ready to get up to speed, challenge other players and become number one on the leaderboard? Come and join Cyber Surfer: Beat&Skateboard!

Mastering the Rhythm

A very important factor in the game is the rhythm, not only to hear the rhythm but also to be able to adapt to it. Different rhythms control the character's skating speed is also different, advance understanding of the rhythm of the game can help players better plan the operation of the game, faster forward to the end.

Finger coordination ability

Players need to control the character at all times to glide, avoid obstacles and collect gold coins, so players need to have good finger coordination and be able to switch their fingers at any time to achieve flexible gliding.

Recognition of graphics

The game has many obstacles and gold coins and other props, players need to identify these graphics in time to decide how to act. Sometimes these props will be some unique patterns in front, so players need to have the ability to identify and make the right decision in a short time.

Skating skills

Players can also use some sliding skills in the game, such as back sliding, forward sliding, stopping point, etc. These skills can help players to better avoid obstacles or collect gold coins, etc., and move forward quickly to the end.

Improve the use of props

There are some props that can help players to glide better, such as acceleration, spring, etc. Players need to master these acceleration skills, and also need to use these props skillfully in practice to improve the game experience and score.

Collect coins and props

There are many gold coins and props to collect. By collecting gold coins, you can unlock other maps, get other characters, strengthen your skills, etc., while props can help players glide better and avoid obstacles, etc.

Try to encounter music

In the game, as the character slides, the music will also change. Players should try to synchronize their sliding with the music, which can make the game more interesting and challenging.

Make efficient use of resurrection opportunities

In the game, sometimes you will fall into the ditch or run into some obstacles that lead to failure, but the game also provides a certain amount of resurrection opportunities for players to start again. Players should make full use of the resurrection opportunities, don't give up easily, and explore and experience well.

Challenge other players

There are other online players in the game, and players can challenge them to increase their fun and get a higher ranking. One can gain advantage by cheating or tricks, or improve their skating by exchanging tips and experience with other players.

Study the maps

There are many different maps in the game, each with different challenges and difficulties. Players can spend time studying the different maps and mastering them to improve their game and get higher scores.

Cyber Surfer: Beat&Skateboard is a very interesting game that not only has gorgeous graphics and gorgeous sound effects, but also covers a wide range of challenges and difficulties to make the game exciting and entertaining for players. At the same time, the game also requires players to have a good sense of rhythm and finger coordination, and they need to constantly improve their skills and techniques in order to master the game and get high scores on the leaderboard. Finally, we hope that players will have fun and also pay attention to the game time to avoid over-indulgence.
