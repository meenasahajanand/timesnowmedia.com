1.Cookies

A cookie consists mainly of letters and numbers, and it is a file that contains a special identifier.

Cookies can be either session cookies or persistent cookies. Session cookies are temporary and are deleted when the user closes their browser. Persistent cookies, on the other hand, remain on the user's device until they expire or are manually deleted.

Cookies themselves do not contain any personal user information or private information, they are just storage files that are stored on your personal device and do not leave any data on the server and you have full control over them.

For website operations, the website uses cookies to promote personalized content and advertisements that users like, to provide social media interaction features, and to analyze the website's own traffic and data. This allows site administrators to better optimize functionality and improve the user experience. For example, a website may use cookies to analyze a user's language preference so that they do not have to select it each time they visit the site. cookies can also be used to analyze user behavior, such as which pages they visit and how long they stay on a particular page. The data feedback from this information can better improve the design and content of the site

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies, and you can manage your cookie preferences by clicking on the "cookie settings" link at the bottom of any page on our website. Please note that if you disable cookies, certain features of our site may not function properly.

It is worth noting that some users may be concerned that website cookies may cause privacy issues. Here we can assure users that website cookies do not have the function of collecting user privacy, s all cookies can only be used for partial data analysis, and all your actions on the website are protected by relevant laws and regulations.
